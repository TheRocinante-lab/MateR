The binary for R 4.4 is compatible with all versions of R 4.4. It was compiled in R 4.4.3. It will not work in R 4.5 until the RcppEigen package is made compatible with it.

The binary for R 4.3 or lower is not compatible with R 4.4 or R 4.5, but it is with previous versions. It was compiled in R 4.3.1
